'Balatro': Turning Low Resolution into High Praise
+
Wout van Halderen | Playstack
Original English Abstract
English

In this session, Wout van Halderen unpacks the marketing strategy behind Balatro, a roguelike deckbuilder with unconventional visual style that posed significant challenges in traditional marketing channels. The talk details how the team pivoted to focus on the game's streamability and high replay value to drive player engagement and interest. It draws on lessons from The Case of the Golden Idol, emphasizing the importance of demos, influencer partnerships, and community-driven campaigns.

Chinese / 中文

在本场演讲中，Wout van Halderen深入剖析了《Balatro》的营销策略。这是一款roguelike卡组构筑游戏，其非传统视觉风格在传统营销渠道中遇到了显著挑战。演讲详细介绍了团队如何将重点转向游戏的可直播性和高重玩价值，以驱动玩家参与度和兴趣。借鉴了《黄金神像谋杀案》的经验，强调了试玩版、网红合作以及社区驱动活动的重要性。

Key Strategies / 核心策略
English

1. Demo Strategy: The first demo released on Reddit gained modest traction until streamer Northernlion played it, causing the player count to surge to 5,000+ in a single day. The second demo, timed with Steam Next Fest, added 40,000 wishlists despite not being an official part of the event.

2. Influencer Approach: Rather than pursuing major influencers, Playstack targeted mid-sized streamers who specialized in similar games like Slay the Spire. Personalized outreach messages that referenced the streamer's favorite game elements proved highly effective.

3. Community Building: Their Discord server grew to over 10,000 members within weeks. The "Jimbo Invitational" tournament, featuring 24-hour competitive streams, generated authentic content and community engagement.

Chinese / 中文

1. 试玩版策略：首个在Reddit发布的试玩版数据平平，直到主播Northernlion试玩后才爆火，单日玩家突破5000人。第二个试玩版配合Steam新品节，尽管未正式参展，但仍新增4万愿望单。

2. 网红策略：不盲目追求大咖，而是寻找擅长类似游戏（如《杀戮尖塔》）的中小型主播。定制化邀请信息（结合主播喜爱的游戏元素）被证明非常有效。

3. 社区建设：Discord频道几周内破万成员。"Jimbo邀请赛"通过24小时竞赛直播产生真实内容和高参与度。

Critical Quote / 重要引述
English

"This is a roguelike poker that will turn 20 minutes into 3 hours."

Chinese / 中文

"这是一款roguelike扑克，会把20分钟变成3小时。"

"Database-Oriented Design": Why We Built Our MMORPG Inside a Database
+
Tyler Cloutier | Clockwork Labs / SpacetimeDB
Original English Abstract
English

Why build an MMORPG backend entirely inside a database? What does that even mean? In this session, attendees will learn why the developers of BitCraft, a large-scale survival crafting MMORPG, built the game's backend entirely within SpacetimeDB, a relational database system complete with ACID transactions. The developers review the current state of the art in MMORPG backend architecture, break down misconceptions about the real-time performance of databases, and reveal how RDBMS + WebAssembly can massively simplify the development and deployment of large scale multiplayer games.

Chinese / 中文

为什么要把一个MMORPG的后端完全构建在一个数据库内？这到底意味着什么？在这次分享中，参与者将了解BitCraft（一款大规模生存建造MMORPG）的开发者为何选择将游戏后端完全构建在SpacetimeDB（一种包含ACID事务的关系型数据库系统）之内。开发者会回顾当前MMORPG后端架构的现状，打破关于数据库实时性能的误解，并揭示关系型数据库管理系统+Rust + WebAssembly如何能够极大简化大规模多人游戏的开发和部署。

Core Insights / 核心洞见
English

1. Data-Oriented Design Extension: What initially seems like an unconventional idea slowly transforms into a logical and natural extension of data-oriented design and ECS (Entity Component System).

2. Unique Advantages: Database-oriented design offers unique advantages for MMORPGs including simplified development through incremental query evaluation for real-time client updates and multi-version concurrency control (MVCC).

3. Indie-Friendly: The approach enables indie developers to build MMOs by leveraging advanced database techniques that were previously only accessible to large studios.

Chinese / 中文

1. 数据导向设计的延伸：最初看似疯狂的想法逐渐转变为数据导向设计和实体组件系统（ECS）的合乎逻辑且自然的扩展。

2. 独特优势：以数据库为中心的设计为MMORPG提供了独特优势，包括通过增量查询评估简化实时客户端更新的开发，以及多版本并发控制（MVCC）。

3. 独立开发者友好：这种方法使独立开发者能够构建MMO，利用此前只有大型工作室才能使用的高级数据库技术。

Source / 来源

4. Challenge Resolution: Practical solutions for balancing visual fidelity with real-time performance constraints.

Chinese / 中文

2. DLSS集成：深度学习超级采样技术，在保持性能的同时提供电影级画质。

3. 引擎修改：对虚幻引擎5的自定义修改，以支持游戏独特的渲染需求。
